Questions

Frequently asked questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out to our team any time.

What image formats are supported?

Snapshot accepts JPG, JPEG, and PNG files. Images are securely uploaded to Amazon S3 before analysis begins.

How accurate is the object detection?

Amazon Rekognition typically returns confidence scores above 90% for common objects and scenes, which are displayed alongside every detected label.

Can I download the audio narration?

Yes — every generated audio clip is playable directly in the browser and can be saved from the player controls.

Is there a limit on file size?

Images up to 10MB are supported out of the box. Contact us if you need to process larger batches.
